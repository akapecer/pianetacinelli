Cinelli is nothing more than the most introverted and cerebral side of Pietro Giuliano, a digital artisan and multimedia creator. Born in Finale Ligure in 1996, he obtained his high school diploma from a hospitality institute. As a self-taught learner, he mastered software such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe After Effects, and Adobe Premiere. In recent years, he has ventured into the world of programming, acquiring knowledge of languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

A great music enthusiast, Pietro is also active as a music producer and DJ, performing on prestigious consoles across Northern Italy.
